pyFlora

Do you want to keep your plants healthy and happy? Do you want to know the optimal conditions for each plant species? If yes, then pyFlora is the website for you.

pyFlora is a platform where you can find smart sensors that measure and monitor various parameters of your plants, such as soil, moisture, temperature, humidity, reaction and light.

pyFlora will help you take better care of your plants and prevent common problems like overwatering, underwatering, pests, and diseases. pyFlora is the ultimate solution for plant lovers who want to enjoy the beauty and benefits of nature.
